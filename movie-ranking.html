Top 3 Best movies

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this film is a classic tale of crime and family. I love the beautiful images.

The Dark Knight

Christopher Nolan's superhero film is known for its complex characters and moral dilemmas.

The Shawshank Redemption

This film, based on a Stephen King novella, is a powerful story of hope and friendship.
